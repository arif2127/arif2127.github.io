<<<<<<< I hold a academic background, starting with my undergraduate studies at the Institute of Information Technology (IIT) in the University of Dhaka. I pursued and completed a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Kyung Hee University in South Korea, focusing on computer vision and machine learning. During this time, I engaged in diverse academic research project, including multiview 3D reconstruction, object removal utilizing inpainting techniques, and single-view 3D shape reconstruction. Additionally, I have expertise in areas such as semantic segmentation and object detection. Furthermore, I collaborated with the Sun and Space Weather Lab at Kyung Hee University on enhancing the resolution of solar magnetogram data and reconstructing solar coronal parameters from 2D to 3D.
